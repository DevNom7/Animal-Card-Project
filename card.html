Nom's Gecko

Geckos have transparent scales that cover their eyes rather than eyelids.

  • Scientific Name: Gekkonidae
  • Average Length: 3 - 14 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 20 years
  • Habitat: Variety of habitats from deserts to rainforests and even cooler climates.

Geckos are small, mostly nocturnal lizards found on every continent except Antarctica. Geckos feed on a variety of insects, spiders, and other small animals, as well as fruit and nectar. Geckos are an important part of the ecosystem and help to control populations of insects.
